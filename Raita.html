Raita

Description

Raita is a cooling Indian yogurt-based condiment, often served with spicy dishes to balance the heat. It typically includes cucumbers, tomatoes, onions, and various spices.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of plain yogurt

  • 1 cucumber, peeled and diced

  • 1 tomato, diced

  • 1 small onion, finely chopped

  • 1 teaspoon of ground cumin

  • Salt to taste

  • Fresh cilantro or mint for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, whisk the yogurt until smooth.

  2. Add the diced cucumber, tomato, and onion to the yogurt..

  3. Stir in the ground cumin and salt.

  4. Mix everything together until well combined.

  5. Garnish with fresh cilantro or mint.

Storage

Refrigerate raita in an airtight container. It is best consumed within 2 days.
