Homemade Banana Bread

Description

Banana bread recipe is perfect for beginners and seasoned bakers alike. It results in a moist, flavorful loaf that's great for breakfast or as a snack.

Ingredients

  • 3 ripe bananas

  • 1/3 cup melted butter

  • 1 teaspoon baking soda

  • Pinch of salt/li>

  • 3/4 cup sugar

  • 1 large egg, beaten

  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour

  • Cooking spray or additional butter for the pan

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a 9x5 inch loaf pan.

  2. Mash the ripe bananas with a fork in a mixing bowl until smooth..

  3. Stir the melted butter into the mashed bananas.

  4. Add the baking soda and salt to the mixture and stir well.

  5. Mix in the sugar, beaten egg, and vanilla extract.

  6. Gradually add the flour, stirring just until it's incorporated. Be careful not to overmix; a few lumps are okay.

  7. Pour the batter into the prepared loaf pan.

  8. Cool the banana bread in the pan for 10 minutes, then turn it out onto a wire rack to cool completely.

Storage

Once cooled, store the banana bread wrapped tightly in plastic wrap or in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 3 days.

You can also freeze it for longer storage; wrap slices individually in plastic wrap and then place them in a freezer bag or container for up to 3 months.
